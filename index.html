FAQ

Common Questions

Have more questions? We're always happy to help.

💬 Ask on WhatsApp
Are dental implants painful?
+
The procedure is performed under local anaesthesia, so you won't feel pain during the treatment. Post-procedure, there may be mild discomfort for a few days, which is easily managed with prescribed medication. Most patients are pleasantly surprised by how comfortable the experience is.
How long does a smile makeover take?
+
It depends on your specific treatment plan. Simple makeovers with whitening and veneers can be completed in 2–3 appointments over a week. Comprehensive cases involving implants, braces, and multiple procedures may take 3–12 months. We'll give you a precise timeline during your free consultation.
Is teeth whitening safe for my enamel?
+
Yes! Professional whitening performed by our dentists is completely safe. We use clinically approved whitening agents at correct concentrations. Unlike over-the-counter products, professional whitening doesn't damage enamel when done correctly. Temporary sensitivity may occur, but this resolves within a day.
Do you offer EMI options for expensive treatments?
+
Absolutely! We understand that quality dental care should be accessible to everyone. We offer flexible 0% EMI options for major treatments like implants, smile makeovers, and full mouth rehabilitation. Our team will help you choose the best financial plan during your consultation.
How long do dental implants last?
+
With proper care, dental implants can last a lifetime. The titanium implant itself fuses with your jawbone and is permanent. The crown on top typically lasts 10–20 years. Regular check-ups and good oral hygiene are key to maximizing their longevity.
What age is right to start braces or aligners?
+
The ideal age for braces is typically 11–14 years when permanent teeth are in and jaws are still developing. However, there's no age limit — adults can also benefit greatly from braces or clear aligners. Clear aligners are especially popular with working adults as they're nearly invisible.
